Nutrition & Lifestyle for Children

✅ Recommended

  • Breastfeeding for at least 6 months for optimal immunity
  • Fresh home-cooked meals rich in vegetables and whole grains
  • Adequate vitamin D through daily outdoor play
  • Regular sleep schedule — 9–11 hours for school-age children
  • Screen-time limits — max 1–2 hours for children under 12
  • Play, creativity, and social interaction

❌ Avoid or Reduce

  • Ultra-processed junk food and packaged snacks
  • Cold drinks and artificial fruit juices
  • Excess sugar and candy
  • Ice cream during illness (worsens mucus)
  • Over-scheduling and excessive academic pressure
  • Self-medication with antibiotics for viral infections
