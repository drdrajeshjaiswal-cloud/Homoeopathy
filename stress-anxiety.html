What Is Stress & Anxiety?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can homoeopathy replace antidepressants?

Homoeopathy is used alongside conventional treatment and not as a sudden replacement. As symptoms improve, your psychiatrist may taper medications. Never stop psychiatric medications abruptly without medical supervision.

How quickly does homoeopathy work for anxiety?

Many patients notice improved calmness and sleep within 2–4 weeks. Deeper constitutional changes typically unfold over 3–6 months of treatment.

Is homoeopathy safe for children with anxiety?

Yes. Homoeopathy is extremely safe and effective for childhood anxiety, school phobia, ADHD, and behavioural issues — with no risk of the side effects associated with stimulant medications.

Can homoeopathy treat severe depression?

Homoeopathy is most effective for mild to moderate depression. Severe depression, suicidal ideation, or psychosis requires immediate conventional psychiatric care. Homoeopathy can be a valuable complementary approach.
