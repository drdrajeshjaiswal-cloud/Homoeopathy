Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the online treatment package price? +

All online packages include: a detailed case analysis consultation (via WhatsApp chat, voice, or video call), a personalised homoeopathic prescription, all medicines for the full package duration, home delivery anywhere in India, and follow-up reviews as specified per package.

How can treatment be effective without seeing the patient in person? +

Classical homoeopathy relies primarily on understanding the complete symptom picture — mental, emotional, and physical — rather than physical examination. This information is gathered through detailed case forms, WhatsApp conversations, and voice/video calls. Dr. Jaiswal has successfully treated thousands of online patients across India and abroad using this approach.

Are medicines delivered to my home? What is the delivery time? +

Yes. Medicines are dispatched after your prescription is prepared, typically within 2–3 days of consultation. Delivery takes 3–7 working days depending on your location within India. International/NRI patients can use a local homoeopathic pharmacy with the prescription provided.

What payment methods are accepted? +

We accept UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS/RTGS), and cash at the clinic. Once payment is confirmed, treatment begins within 24 hours.

Can I switch from online to in-clinic treatment? +

Absolutely. You are always welcome to visit the clinic in person at Street No 14, Tarnaka, Secunderabad during clinic hours (Mon–Sat, 6:30 PM – 9:00 PM). If you are an existing online patient, please inform Dr. Jaiswal when booking.

How long does homoeopathic treatment take? +

Treatment duration varies by condition. Acute conditions (cold, fever, infections) may improve within days to weeks. Chronic conditions (psoriasis, PCOS, kidney stones, arthritis) typically require 3–12 months. Dr. Jaiswal will give you a realistic timeline during the initial consultation based on your specific case.

What is the refund policy if I am not satisfied? +

Cancellations before treatment begins (no medicines dispatched) are eligible for a full refund within 7 working days. Once consultation has been conducted or medicines dispatched, refunds are not available. Please see our full Cancellation & Refund Policy for details.
