What conditions can be treated with homoeopathy?
Homoeopathy is effective for both acute and chronic conditions including joint disorders, skin problems, allergies, respiratory issues, digestive disorders, women's health problems, stress and anxiety, pediatric conditions, and many more. How long does treatment take?

Dr. Jaiswal will provide a realistic timeframe during your consultation based on your specific case.
Are there any side effects?
Homoeopathic medicines are prepared from natural substances in highly diluted forms and are completely safe with no side effects. They are gentle, non-toxic, and can be safely used by people of all ages including infants, pregnant women, and elderly patients.
