3. Conditions Requiring Urgent Medical Attention

Homoeopathy may not be suitable as the primary treatment for the following — please seek immediate conventional medical care:

  • Conditions requiring surgery (fractures, appendicitis, acute obstruction)
  • Severe infections requiring antibiotics (bacterial meningitis, sepsis)
  • Mental health crises (suicidal ideation, psychosis)
  • Advanced malignancies requiring oncological management
