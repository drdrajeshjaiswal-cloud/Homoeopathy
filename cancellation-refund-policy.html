Cancellation & Refund Policy

Effective Date: 17 March 2026

At Dr. Jaiswal's Homoeopathy, we are committed to transparent and fair practices. Please read this policy carefully before booking a consultation or purchasing a treatment package. By proceeding with a payment, you agree to the terms set out below.

1. Consultation Cancellations

In-Clinic Consultations

We understand that appointments may need to be rescheduled due to unforeseen circumstances. Our cancellation policy for in-clinic consultations is as follows:

Cancellation Timing Refund Eligibility
Cancelled 24+ hours before appointment Full Rescheduling — appointment moved at no charge
Cancelled less than 24 hours before Rescheduling considered — subject to availability and discretion
No-show without prior notice Not eligible — consultation fee forfeited

To cancel or reschedule an in-clinic appointment, please call or WhatsApp us at +91 99851 88831 at least 24 hours in advance.

Online / WhatsApp Consultations

For online consultations booked and paid in advance:

  • Cancellations made before the consultation begins are eligible for a full refund or rescheduling.
  • Once a consultation has commenced — even partially — the consultation fee is considered utilised and is non-refundable.
  • If a technical issue on our end prevents the consultation from proceeding, a full refund or alternative appointment will be arranged promptly.

2. Treatment Package Cancellations

Dr. Jaiswal's Homoeopathy offers online treatment packages (2-month, 4-month, and 6-month) that include consultations and medicine delivery. The following terms apply:

Scenario Refund
Cancellation before treatment begins (no medicines dispatched) Full refund within 7 working days
Cancellation after medicines dispatched but before first consultation No refund
Cancellation after first consultation completed No refund
Cancellation after 50% or more of package period elapsed No refund

Please note: Homoeopathic treatment requires consistency over time for optimal results. We strongly encourage patients to complete their prescribed treatment course rather than discontinuing mid-way. If you have concerns about your treatment progress, please discuss them with Dr. Jaiswal before requesting a cancellation.

3. Medicine Returns

Due to the nature of homoeopathic medicines and patient safety:

  • Medicines once dispatched and received cannot be returned or exchanged under any circumstances.
  • In the rare event of a dispensing error on our part, we will replace the affected medicines at no cost. Please contact us within 48 hours of receiving medicines with photographic evidence.
  • Medicines damaged in transit must be reported within 48 hours of delivery with photographs of the damaged packaging.

4. Refund Process

Approved refunds will be processed as follows:

  • Refunds are issued via the same payment method used for the original transaction (UPI, bank transfer, etc.).
  • Processing time: 5–7 working days after refund approval.
  • Cash payments made at the clinic will be refunded in cash at the clinic only.
  • Refund requests must be submitted by contacting us directly via WhatsApp or phone — not through third-party platforms.

5. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following circumstances:

  • Dissatisfaction with treatment outcomes — homoeopathy works over time and individual responses vary
  • Failure to follow prescribed dietary or lifestyle recommendations
  • Discontinuation of treatment due to personal reasons after the consultation has been conducted
  • Medicines already dispatched and delivered
  • Completed consultations, whether in-clinic or online
  • Packages where 50% or more of the treatment period has elapsed

6. Appointment Rescheduling

We are happy to reschedule appointments without any charge, subject to availability. To reschedule:

  • Contact us at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment
  • WhatsApp: +91 99851 88831
  • Call: +91 99851 88831
  • Clinic hours for rescheduling: Mon–Sat, 6:30 PM – 9:00 PM

7. Force Majeure

In exceptional circumstances beyond our control — such as natural disasters, severe illness, or other force majeure events — appointments will be rescheduled without any penalty. If rescheduling is not possible, a full refund will be issued.

8. Disputes

We hope to resolve all concerns amicably. If you are dissatisfied with any aspect of our service or this policy, please contact us directly. We will make every reasonable effort to find a fair resolution within 7 working days of your complaint.

Any unresolved disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the courts in Hyderabad, Telangana, India.
