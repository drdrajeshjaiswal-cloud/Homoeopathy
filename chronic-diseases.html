What Is Chronic Diseases?

Chronic diseases are long-term health conditions that persist for months or years, often requiring continuous management. In today's stressful lifestyle, conditions like diabetes, hypertension, and thyroid disorders have become increasingly common — affecting millions of families across India.

While conventional medicine excels at controlling acute symptoms, it often falls short in addressing the underlying imbalances that drive chronic disease. Patients find themselves on multiple medications for decades, with side effects compounding over time.

Constitutional homoeopathy offers a fundamentally different approach — treating the whole person, not just the numbers on a blood report.
